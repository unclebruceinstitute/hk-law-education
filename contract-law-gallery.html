LAWS6021 Principles of Contract — Offer & Replies to Inquiries

Harvey v Facey [1893] AC 552 (PC)

1

🏗️ 合約成立的三大支柱

The Three Pillars of Contract Formation

合約必須具備三大要素：合意（Consensus ad idem）、建立法律關係的意圖（Animus contrahendi）、對價（Consideration）。三根支柱缺一不可。

A contract requires three essential elements: (1) Consensus ad idem — a meeting of minds; (2) Animus contrahendi — intention to create legal relations; (3) Consideration. All three pillars must be present.

2

📜 要約的定義

Definition of Offer

要約是一個明確的表示，合理的人會理解為「一經承諾即受約束」。條款必須確定或可確定。根據 Halsbury's Laws of Hong Kong 的定義。

An offer is "an expression of willingness to be bound to a contract on terms certain or capable of being rendered certain", as defined in Halsbury's Laws of Hong Kong.

3

📨 Harvey v Facey — 買方的查詢

Harvey v Facey — The Buyer's Inquiry

Harvey 向 Facey 發出電報：「你願意以多少錢出售 Bumper Hall Pen？」這只是一個查詢（Inquiry），並非要約（Offer）。

Harvey sent a telegram to Facey: "Will you sell us Bumper Hall Pen? State lowest price." This was merely an inquiry, not an offer capable of acceptance.

4

📝 Facey 的回覆 — 僅是資訊

Facey's Reply — Information Only

Facey 回覆：「最低售價為 £1,000」。法院裁定這僅是提供價格資訊，並非表示願意出售的要約。一個合理的人不會認為這是承諾。

Facey replied: "Lowest price for Bumper Hall Pen is £1,000." The Privy Council held this was merely providing information, not an offer to sell. A reasonable person would not understand it as a willingness to be bound.

5

⚖️ 樞密院的判決

The Privy Council's Judgment

樞密院裁定：無要約即無合約。Facey 的回覆不構成要約，因此 Harvey 的「接受」無效。這是回覆查詢與要約的關鍵區別。

The Privy Council ruled: "No offer, no contract." Facey's reply did not constitute an offer, therefore Harvey's purported "acceptance" was ineffective. This establishes the crucial distinction between a reply to inquiry and an offer.

6

🧪 客觀測試 — 合理人標準

The Objective Test — Reasonable Person Standard

法院採用客觀測試：一個站在受要約人立場的合理人，會如何理解該表示？不探究當事人的主觀意圖，而是以外在行為判斷。

The court applies an objective test: how would a reasonable person in the position of the offeree understand the statement? The court does not investigate subjective intention but judges by outward conduct.

7

📚 語境決定性質

Context Determines the Nature

同樣的文字在不同語境下可能有不同法律效果。Harvey 案的回覆是資訊，但 Carlill v Carbolic Smoke Ball Co 案的廣告卻構成單邊要約。Everything depends on the words and context.

The same words may have different legal effects in different contexts. In Harvey, the reply was mere information; yet in Carlill v Carbolic Smoke Ball Co, an advertisement constituted a unilateral offer. Everything depends on the words employed and the context.

8

📌 重點總結

Key Takeaways

① 要約必須顯示受約束的意圖 ② 回覆查詢僅是資訊 ③ 採用客觀合理人測試 ④ 語境決定法律效果 ⑤ 區分要約與邀約要約

① An offer must show willingness to be bound ② A reply to inquiry is mere information ③ Apply the objective reasonable person test ④ Context determines legal effect ⑤ Distinguish offer from invitation to treat
